# Privacy Policy

**BreathTime**
Last updated: April 6, 2026

## 1. Introduction

Nexona ("we", "us", "our") operates the BreathTime application ("App"). This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your rights regarding your data.

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Data Stored Locally on Your Device

The following data is stored **only on your device** using local storage (SharedPreferences) and is **never transmitted** to our servers:

- **Practice history** — records of completed breathing sessions (practice type, date, duration)
- **Language preference** — your selected app language
- **Theme preference** — your selected appearance mode (light, dark, or system)
- **Sound preference** — whether sounds are enabled or disabled
- **Practice completion count** — used to determine when to show an app review prompt

This data remains on your device and is deleted when you uninstall the App.

### 2.2 Crash and Error Reports

We use **Firebase Crashlytics** (provided by Google) to collect crash reports and error logs. This data may include:

- Device type and operating system version
- App version
- Crash stack traces and error messages
- General device state at the time of a crash

This information is used solely to identify and fix bugs. It does **not** include personal information, practice history, or your preferences.

### 2.3 Remote Configuration

We use **Firebase Remote Config** (provided by Google) to deliver app configuration updates such as version update notifications. This service may collect:

- Basic device and app metadata (platform, app version)

No personal data is collected through this service.

### 2.4 What We Do NOT Collect

- We do **not** collect your name, email address, or any personal identifiers
- We do **not** require account creation or login
- We do **not** track your location
- We do **not** use advertising or analytics tracking
- We do **not** share or sell any data to third parties
- We do **not** collect any health or biometric data

## 3. Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services, each with their own privacy policies:

| Service | Purpose | Privacy Policy |
|---------|---------|----------------|
| Firebase Crashlytics | Crash reporting | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |
| Firebase Remote Config | App configuration | https://firebase.google.com/support/privacy |
| In-App Review (Apple/Google) | App Store review prompt | Governed by Apple/Google privacy policies |

## 4. Data Retention

- **Local data** is retained on your device until you clear the app data or uninstall the App.
- **Crash reports** are retained by Firebase according to Google's data retention policies (typically 90 days).

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App does not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. The App does not require any personal information to function.

## 6. Your Rights

Since we do not collect personal data on our servers, there is no personal data for us to access, modify, or delete. All user data is stored locally on your device and is under your control.

If you are located in the European Economic Area (EEA), you retain all rights under the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR). You can delete all locally stored data by uninstalling the App.

## 7. Security

We take reasonable measures to ensure the App is secure. All local data is stored using standard platform storage mechanisms. Crash data is transmitted to Firebase over encrypted connections.

## 8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above. Continued use of the App constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
